Onions


We Farm proudly offers a selection of organic onions, cultivated with sustainable and eco-friendly practices. Our onions are grown without synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or GMOs, making them a healthier option for you and better for the planet. We enhance our soil with natural compost, practice crop rotation, and use natural pest control methods, resulting in nutrient-rich and flavorful onions. Our varieties include red, yellow, white, and sweet onions, each picked at peak ripeness to ensure the best taste and nutrition. You can purchase our organic onions at our farm store, local farmers' markets, or through our convenient weekly delivery service.
